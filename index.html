About Us

Dedicated to creating positive change in communities across India through education, healthcare, and sustainable development.

Wonder Woman Brand Ambassador of Volunteering

Perumalla Gowri Sirisha

"Problems pale in comparison to the resolve to help others."

A volunteer is someone who steps forward voluntarily—driven by a selfless spirit of service and an impartial attitude—to extend a helping hand. Through her extraordinary service, a woman leading an ordinary life has demonstrated that, when faced with the determination to support those in distress, one's own poverty is merely a minor obstacle.

Moved by the suffering of others, weary of age-old social inequalities, and deeply affected by the sight of lives ravaged by financial ruin, Sirisha resolved to stand as a pillar of support. As the daughter of Perumalla Suryanarayana—a prominent RTI activist—she inherited a deep sensitivity to human suffering and profound empathy for victims of injustice.

Our Vision

A world where every individual has access to justice, healthcare, education, and dignified livelihood — regardless of gender, caste, or economic background.

  • Women Empowerment: Free tailoring centers, self-defense training, and legal aid for women.
  • Health for All: Blood donation camps, COVID relief, and accessible medical support.
  • Environmental Sustainability: Tree planting drives and awareness campaigns.

Our Mission

"To serve as a pillar of support for the distressed, the illiterate, and the elderly — ensuring their rights, dignity, and well-being."

  • Legal Aid: Para-Legal services through District Legal Services Authority to ensure justice for all.
  • Food Security: Distributed essentials to 50,000+ families during COVID-19 lockdown.
  • Youth Development: Personality development programs and awards for young volunteers.

Selfless Service

Driven by a spirit of voluntary service, putting others' needs before our own — regardless of personal circumstances.

Social Justice

Standing against age-old inequalities and ensuring every individual has access to their rightful entitlements and legal aid.

Women's Safety

Empowering women through self-defense training, legal protection, and economic independence via skill development.

Environmental Care

Planting thousands of saplings and fighting pollution — because caring for nature is caring for the future.
